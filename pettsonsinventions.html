Pettson's Inventions 4

Info

  • Engine: Unity
  • Languages: C#, XML
  • Platform: Android, iOS
  • Time frame: 15 weeks
  • Team Size: 6

Overview

During my internship at Filimundus AB, I primarily contributed to the development of Pettson's Inventions 4, a commercially released puzzle game aimed at children.

The game is about Findus and the player fixing up Pettson's Santa Machine. To do this, the player must build contraptions to help the lovable Muckles, who lend a hand in return.

The game's codebase builds on previous titles in the series. My fellow programming intern, Bavo Devos, and I worked closely together to adapt legacy systems and develop new features on top of them.

Contributions

  • ► Design and documentation of our new trunk-based branching strategy using Git.
  • ► Tool shed / HUB swipe and zoom navigation with parallax effect.
  • ► Drag and drop Santa dressup minigame.
  • ► Data-driven cutscene creator / manager.
  • ► Optimization using Unity's Profiler.
  • ► Game save system and progression between sessions.
  • ► Managing of game builds for Android and iOS
  • ► Management of game builds for Android and iOS
  • ► Localization using XML.

Reflections

It was amazing working on a full-fledged production! I had a lot of freedom to explore and learn different tools and processes during this project. Specifically, I really deepened my knowledge in version control, profiling, and mobile development as a whole.

I wish however that I could have gone more in-depth into the localization process. I only had time to scratch the surface here and would love to explore it further in future projects.

I'm very proud of the project and the development team at Filimunds AB. We put our all into making this project with the skills we had. We also created such a positive and supportive work environment together. I miss you all dearly!
