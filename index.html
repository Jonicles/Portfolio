So why choose me?

Knowledge Sponge

I like to think of myself as a "knowledge sponge", soaking up knowledge from anywhere and anyone. I believe you can always learn something, even from the unlikeliest of places, if you are open and willing to listen. Frequently I find myself asking people within other disciplines questions and being able to apply it to my own.

Efficiency Seeker

I'm always looking for ways to improve the efficiency of my work without compromising quality. I want there to be as little friction as possible between thinking of something and creating it. I'm constantly on the lookout for better software, workflows, and equipment. I plan out my work diligently to stay organized and efficient.

Problem Solver

Being able to solve problems is an essential skill for any programmer since that is basically what all coding is. I've always enjoyed solving puzzles, and when I became a shift manager, I really got to hone this skill. There were always results you could improve, whether it be cleanliness, speed, quality, etc... I would often spend my time analyzing the logistics of the restaurant to find bottlenecks and solutions to them.

Doer

Most of all I'm a doer. If there is something I want to do, I will find a way to do it. If I need something done, I don't say "I can't do that", I say "How do you do that and how do you do it well?". I research, I analyze, I implement, and I iterate. I never want to feel like I'm standing still, and I strive to be a lifelong learner.
